Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.


    The Yummiest Places to Dine
  1. Cup Bab
  2. Dae Jang Kum
  3. Sunny Side
  4. Sushi Spot
  5. Himawari

Cup Bab has two locations.One is alongside Beach Road Garapan and the other at San Jose but they only do takeouts. This restaurant serves good korean food at an affordable price. You won't regret taking a stop here.

    What I love:
  • Short waiting times
  • Affordable Prices
  • Many delicious foods to choose from
